Beef Bourguignon

Description

Classic French beef stew for cold days! Haven't made it before but looks awesome.

Ingredients

  • Beef Chuck
  • Flour
  • Bacon
  • Onion
  • Carrots
  • Celery
  • Mushroom
  • Garlic
  • Cognac
  • Tomato Paste
  • Dry Red Wine
  • Beef Stock
  • Brown Sugar
  • Thyme Leaves

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 deg
  2. Cut beef into chunks and season with salt and pepper then flour and set aside
  3. Slice bacon into small pieces, brown until crispy in a dutch oven and set aside
  4. Add beef to bacon fat and brown on all sides. Remove and set aside
  5. Add onions, carrots, celery and mushrooms to pot. Cook until softened. Add garlic and cook until aromatic. Remove vegetables and put in the beef bowl
  6. Deglaze pan with cognac and reduce by half
  7. Stir in tomato paste and then add wine, stock, thyme, brown sugar and salt & pepper
  8. Return bacon, beef and vegetables to the pot, cover and place in oven
  9. Cook for 2 hours in preheated oven until beef is fork-tender
  10. Serve garnished with parsley over mashed potatoes
