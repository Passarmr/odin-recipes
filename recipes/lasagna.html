Lasagna

Description

This is my go-to lasagna recipe. It's awesome. Takes about 5-6 hours all in but worth it in the end.

Ingredients

  • Sausage
  • Ground Beef
  • Mushrooms
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Tomato Paste
  • Crushed Tomatoes
  • Mozzarella
  • Ricotta
  • Pasta
  • Parmasan

Steps

  1. Brown sausage and beef
  2. Add mushroom, onion and garlic and sweat
  3. Add tomato paste, crushed tomatoes and season with salt, pepper and italian herbs
  4. Simmer for 2 hours
  5. Cook lasagna noodles until al dente
  6. Layer lasagna. Sauce, Pasta, Ricotta, Sauce, Pasta, Ricotta, Sauce, Pasta, Sauce
  7. Top with Mozzarella and shave parmasan on top
  8. Bake in 350 degree oven, first half covered with foil
