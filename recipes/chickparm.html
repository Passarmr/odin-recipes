Chicken Parmasan

Description

Crispy awesome chicken parmasan! A classic for any big meal.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breasts
  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Bread Crumbs
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Mozzarella
  • Parmasan
  • Basil

Steps

  1. Slice chicken breasts in half
  2. Cover chicken in flour, egg and then bread crumbs and set aside
  3. When finished, fry chicken in vat of vegetable oil
  4. Top cooked chicken with marinara sauce, 2 or 3 pats of fresh mozz and shave fresh parmasan over them
  5. Heat in a 400 deg oven until cheese is melted and slightly browned
  6. Serve with more sauce and pasta on the side
